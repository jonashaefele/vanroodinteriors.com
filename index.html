Conran Interiors

Interior design, art direction and original artwork

A new residential development and Curzon cinema located in Old Street, London.

Produce a colour scheme and source furniture for 12 rental apartments of varying size, overseeing all aspects of interior design, procurement and installation.

I was commissioned by Cuckooz Apartments to lead the interior design of their newest and largest property, as part of a collaboration with well established procurement specialists Conran Interiors.

Retaining their playful & youthful brand aesthetic I created a sophisticated theme including unique mid-century pieces and high quality accent coloured furniture, to balance with the existing neutral marble interiors and white washed walls.

Launching in early 2019, this landmark property’s interior has bene widely praised, thus creating desirable homes for their target market.

Photoshoot Art Direction

The UK's largest boutique Serviced Apartment operator.

Stylise and shoot over 30 apartments across 14 properties in various London locations, capturing the space, versatility, location, amenities and uniqueness of each apartment.

As part of a wider rebrand project, the photography was primarily required for a new, rebranded, image led e-commerce website, designed to promote the eclectic nature of the portfolio, highlight the benefits, and increase bookings.

Working within a limited budget and tight timescales, I sourced a series of versatile props for the entire shoot to replacing the previous somewhat sterile photography, and create lifestyle themes throughout.

A balance of 5-6 images per apartment was struck to minimise load time, while highlighting the uniqueness of each property, and it’s facileness by capturing colours, materials, work spaces, large bedrooms, fully equipped kitchens & dining spaces.

Photography by Alex Hare.
