Born and based in London, with qualifications in Graphic Design and Interior Design I loosely describe myself as a Conceptual Creative, working with branding agencies, marketing teams and individual clients to provide design concepts, strategy, management and roll-out.

Influenced by architecture, construction, textiles and anthropology, I believe good design solutions come from persistence, planning, teamwork and encouragement.
